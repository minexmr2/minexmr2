Brief introduction to the pool

This pool's owner is absolutely independent on previous minexmr.com pool's owner. The goal is to create a much more elegant, spartan, robust incarnation of legendary old-school Monero mining pool, preserving well known name. There is no single boring detail in web-UI. No registration, no cookies, no personal data collection. See what garbage violates your privacy do you see in top 3 miningpoolstats.stream/monero: nanopool.org, really?!

One may ask: why I have to trust in this brand new pool? Just because payment threshold is as low as 0.01XMR, it becomes possible because of Monero tail emission allows transaction fees to be as low as 0.00004XMR. You simply can't lose significant Monero funds by definition even in most worse case!

We have decided not to use social media like Twitter, Reddit, Facebook, TikTok, etc, to broadcast minexmr2.com online status and current technical works. Instead we use advanced approach based on GitHub, GitLab README.md files and issues. In a case of this service unavailable feel free to do one of the following:

  • Check either GitHub or GitLab project's README file for online status
  • Write email to official support: minexmr2@tutanota.com
  • Create new GitHub project's issue or GitLab project's issue.
Whatever happens, don't worry about your pending XMR funds: pool is beyond pretty strong configured ufw firewall and haproxy shield. Also this pool has upstream server, highly isolated counterpart that performs all the payment processing. Your pending balance is being duplicated on both edge and upstream servers via an encrypted openvpn tunnel. Of course, all the services' ports (where possible) are non-standard and secret.

This pool is intended for those people who do respect the privacy and care about their personal data. One can consider this pool as intermediate step to most sophisticated decentralized p2pool or a quick robust solution where he can't spend precious time to install, deploy and maintain his own p2pool instance (actually, it partially uses p2pool inside). MINEXMR2 is a pretty good alternative to both MINEXMR and p2pool.

Support email for manual payouts: minexmr2@tutanota.com.

Pool and site implementation of minexmr2.com is open source and mirrored on both GitHub, GitLab. It is built against Monero Fluorine Fermi, Major Point Release 1 v0.18.1.0.
